What we offer.

A handful of the details our offers tend to spell out in plainer language than most. Full list lives in the candidate packet; ask for it.

01 · Compensation

Transparent bands, published.

Every job posting lists the salary band. No haggling based on prior comp — we don't ask.

02 · Equity

Early-exercise, 10-yr PTE.

ISOs with early-exercise provisions and a 10-year post-termination window. We think vesting cliffs are insulting.

03 · Health

100% premiums, dependents too.

Medical, dental, vision. We cover the full premium for you and your dependents on the PPO plan.

04 · Time

Four weeks, actually taken.

Four weeks minimum PTO and a company shutdown between Christmas and New Year. We notice when people don't take their days.

05 · Parental

16 weeks, any parent.

16 weeks of fully-paid parental leave for any primary or secondary caregiver. Phased return-to-work supported.

06 · Travel

Floor visits, two per year.

Every teammate shadows two inspections per year, anywhere in the network, fully expensed. Yes, even if your job is backend.
