— Pricing questions

Honest answers.

If there's something else you want straight from us before procurement, just ask.

Why per-site and not per-user?

Because quality data is an operational concern, not a seat count. Your night-shift supervisor should see exactly what your ops director sees. We don't want pricing to make that harder.

What counts as a “site”?

A distinct physical location with its own operational rubric, an airport terminal, a hospital building, a retail location, a cleanroom suite. Ask us about edge cases; we've probably seen yours.

Can we run this with our own inspectors?

Yes. The platform is designed for your staff, a contractor's staff, or IQS-operated inspector crews. The independence happens in the data model, not only in the inspector's org chart.

Is there a free trial?

We run a paid 30-day pilot on 2-3 representative sites. That's how we make sure everyone is serious and that we learn your rubric properly. The pilot cost rolls into your first annual agreement.

Do you have a procurement-friendly agreement?

Yes. Enterprise plans come with a pre-negotiated MSA, DPA, and security addendum. We'll work through your vendor-onboarding process with a named contact on our side, and we can turn around most redlines in under a week.

What does implementation look like?

2-4 weeks for Operator and Portfolio. 6-10 weeks for Enterprise with private deployment. A named implementation engineer runs framework configuration, integrations, and inspector training.
