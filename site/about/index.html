About IQS Flow

The independent quality layer for multi-vendor operations.

Built by operators and engineers who watched monthly reports say everything was fine, and then walked the floor and saw otherwise.

— The story

We started because the reports were lying.

For twenty years, Venice Collier managed facility services for airlines, hospitals, and retail chains. Every month the vendor reports said the same thing: 98% quality, zero findings, all green. Every month the reality on the floor said something else, a concourse restroom that had not been touched in three shifts, a cleanroom with particulate counts no one caught until QA did, a compliance finding that cost the renewal.

The gap between the report and the reality was never malicious. It was structural. When the vendor writes the scorecard, the scorecard reflects the vendor. So we built the platform we wished we'd had, one that captures ground truth from the field, scores it independently, and hands your vendor the same data you see.

IQS Flow is headquartered in Atlanta. We are a small team of operators, engineers, and inspectors. We keep it that way on purpose, because this work requires that the people building the product also walk the floor.

— What we believe

Three principles. No exceptions.

01 · Independence

Never the same party

The hand that does the work should never grade the work. Our inspectors don't work for your vendors. Full stop.

02 · Evidence

Photo, GPS, timestamp

Every inspection carries its own proof. No photo, no GPS, no log, no inspection. We publish the rubric.

03 · Transparency

Everyone sees the same thing

Your vendor sees what you see. No separate dashboards, no spin, no surprises. Same data, same time.
